Our Services

We offer a variety of dog sitting services to meet your needs. Our professional dog sitters will take good care of your furry friends while you're away.

Dog Walking

Our dog walkers will take your dog on a 30-minute walk, twice a day. We'll make sure your dog gets plenty of exercise and fresh air.

Price: $25 per day

Overnight Dog Sitting

Our dog sitters will stay overnight at your home to take care of your dog. We'll feed your dog, give them fresh water, and let them out to use the bathroom.

Price: $75 per night

Dog Daycare

We offer dog daycare services where your dog can socialize with other dogs and play all day. We'll make sure your dog is safe and has plenty of fun.

Price: $50 per day
